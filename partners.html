DOCFILMPOOL e.V.
Creative and collective filmmaking in times of Covid-19 and beyond
The registered association DOCFILMPOOL e.V. was founded by members of the German Association of Documentary Filmmakers (AG DOK) during the "Covid-19 shutdown" to enable new ways of collaboration between filmmakers. A steadily growing collection of digital footage is being created for DOCFILMPOOL e.V.. The pool consists of material created by both members and non-members and is available under specific license conditions. DOCFILMPOOL e.V. provides a legal framework in which a variety of audio-visual projects using this footage can be created and published.

DOK.fest Munich is the first major German documentary film festival to be held online due to the Covid-19 crisis. All three competition series take place as planned: DOK.international, DOK.deutsch and DOK.horizonte - and all 14 prizes are awarded. 121 films from 42 countries will be presented in the online edition of the festival, therefore showing the bulk of the 159 films that had already been confirmed for the regular festival.
